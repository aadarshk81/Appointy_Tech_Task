Viral image shows scale of melting ice in Greenland

A photo showing sled dogs wading through water ankle-deep on top of a melting ice sheet in Greenland has gone viral. The picture taken by Steffen Olsen from the Centre for Ocean and Ice at the Danish Meteorogical Institute shows The scale of melting ice in Greenland. Olsen said The ice sheet beneath the water was 1.2 metres thick.
